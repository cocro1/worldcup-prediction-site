## 方法论

### Bayesian 收缩 Poisson 模型（shrinkage=3）

**核心参数：**
- 锦标赛场均进球（baseline）：λ_avg = 2.50
- 收缩系数：shrinkage = 3

**模型公式：**
```
att_shrunk[i] = (n[i] × att_raw[i] + shrinkage × λ_avg) / (n[i] + shrinkage)
def_shrunk[i] = (n[i] × def_raw[i] + shrinkage × λ_avg) / (n[i] + shrinkage)
att_raw[i] = GF[i] / n[i] / λ_avg
def_raw[i] = GA[i] / n[i] / λ_avg
λ_home[i] = att_shrunk[home] × def_shrunk[away]
λ_away[i] = att_shrunk[away] × def_shrunk[home]
P(X=k) = exp(-λ) × λ^k / k!
P(>2.5) = 1 - Σ P(X≤2)
```
